about me

Background

Hi! I go by Francois.

I was born in Shanghai, China where I lived for 5 years. I first moved to Bangkok Thailand where I lived for 2 years and where my brother was born. Next was Seoul, Korea where I lived for 5 years. Finally, I made it to Maryland, America where I am now.

Currently, I am a Mechanical Engineering undergraduate at UMD. I am in our Terrapin Rockets Club as a member of both the Two-Stage and Airbrake sub teams and I am a Manager at our Sandbox Makerspace in Iribe.

I enjoy Taekwondo, art, and volleyball in my free time.
